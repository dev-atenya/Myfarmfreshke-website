How it started

Born from the farm

MyFarmFresh began with a simple question: why should fresh, nutritious produce go to waste when there is a way to preserve it naturally, without chemicals, and make it available to everyone?

Founded in 2021 in Kiambu County, we started by drying surplus mango and pineapple from a single partner farm. Word spread quickly. Customers loved the taste, the purity, and the fact that they could get top-quality Kenyan-grown produce that lasted.

Today we work with a network of over 15 local farms, process four categories of produce, and supply households, retailers, and food businesses across Kenya.
