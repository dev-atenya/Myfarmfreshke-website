Dried fruits

Sun-dried and air-dried to lock in natural sweetness, vitamins, and minerals — with absolutely no added sugars, sulphites, or preservatives. Our dried fruits are perfect as healthy snacks, granola toppers, baking ingredients, or trail mix additions.

Available in retail pouches (100g, 250g, 500g) and bulk bags for wholesale buyers.

Varieties available

Mango Pineapple Banana chips Papaya Passion fruit Guava Jackfruit

Dried vegetables

Carefully dried at low temperatures to retain their natural colour, flavour, and nutritional value. Our dried vegetables are ideal for soups, stews, rice dishes, smoothies, and meal prep — rehydrate in minutes or use straight from the pouch as a crunchy snack.

Great for households, restaurants, hotels, and food manufacturers looking for consistent quality.

Varieties available

Kale Spinach Sun-dried tomatoes Carrots Beetroot Zucchini Red capsicum Mushrooms

Fresh fruits & vegetables

When you need farm-fresh rather than dried, we can supply fresh produce directly from our partner farms. Ideal for households, juice bars, hotels, schools, and bulk buyers who want to cut out the middleman and get truly fresh produce at fair prices.

Fresh orders are fulfilled on request based on seasonal availability and farm schedules. Contact us to discuss your regular supply needs.
