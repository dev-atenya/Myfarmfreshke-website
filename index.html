How it works

From farm to your door

Every batch follows the same careful four-step journey to ensure quality you can taste.

1

Harvest

Sourced fresh from our network of local Kenyan farms at peak ripeness.

2

Clean & sort

Each batch is hand-sorted and washed to remove any substandard produce.

3

Dry naturally

Low-heat solar or air drying preserves nutrients, flavour, and colour.

4

Pack & deliver

Sealed in resealable pouches and delivered to your door or picked up locally.
